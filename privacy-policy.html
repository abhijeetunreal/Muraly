Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Muraly. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection and use of information when you use our free augmented reality mural visualization service.

By using Muraly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We do not collect personal information for commercial purposes, and your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

Camera and Media Access

Muraly requires access to your device's camera to provide augmented reality visualization. Camera access is requested only when you actively use the AR features. We do not record, store, or transmit your camera feed to our servers. All camera processing occurs locally on your device.

Uploaded Images

When you upload images to visualize as murals, these images are processed entirely on your device. Images may be temporarily stored in your browser's local storage for session persistence, but they are never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

Session Data

When you create or join an AR session, a session identifier is generated to enable peer-to-peer connections. This identifier is used solely for establishing connections between devices and is not linked to your personal information.

Browser and Device Information

Like most web applications, Muraly may automatically collect certain technical information such as browser type, device type, screen resolution, and operating system. This information is used to optimize the service and ensure compatibility.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the AR visualization service
  • Enable peer-to-peer connections for multi-device sharing
  • Improve user experience and service functionality
  • Ensure technical compatibility across different devices and browsers
  • Maintain session state for your convenience

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purposes. Muraly is a free, non-profit service.

Data Storage

Local Storage

Muraly uses your browser's local storage to save session data, uploaded images, and preferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Peer-to-Peer Connections

When sharing sessions between devices, connections are established directly between devices using peer-to-peer technology. Video streams and session data are transmitted directly between devices and are not routed through our servers.

Third-Party Services

Muraly uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • PeerJS: Used for establishing peer-to-peer connections between devices. Please refer to PeerJS Privacy Policy for their data practices.
  • Discovery Service: A service that helps discover available AR sessions. Session identifiers are temporarily stored to enable session discovery.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the service functionality described above.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we may have collected
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke camera or media permissions at any time through your browser settings
  • Clear local storage data through your browser settings
  • Discontinue use of the service at any time

To exercise these rights, you can clear your browser's local storage or contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Muraly is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
