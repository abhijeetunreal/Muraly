Description of Service

Muraly is a free, web-based augmented reality application that allows users to:

  • Upload and visualize images as murals on real surfaces using AR technology
  • Adjust, scale, and position virtual murals in real-time
  • Share AR sessions with multiple devices simultaneously
  • Record timelapse or video of mural visualization sessions
  • Save and load session configurations

The service is provided free of charge and operates entirely within your web browser. No installation or account creation is required.
